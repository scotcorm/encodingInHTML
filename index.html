Seafood Jambalaya

Traditional recipe from a family collection labeled: "Cajun Cookin'"

Jambalaya is a favorite Louisiana dish, and adding seafood instead of the usual chicken, is a luxury touch.

Serves 6 to 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. shrimp (peeled)
  • 1 lb. crabmeat
  • 1 doz. oysters
  • 1 ib. smoked sausage (sliced)
  • 3 c. rice
  • 1 sm. can tomato sauce
  • 1 lg. onion chopped
  • 2/3 c. parsley (chopped)
  • 2/3 c. shallots (chopped)
  • 2/3 c. bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2/3 c. cooking oil

Method

  1. Boil rice- set aside.
  2. Saute' onion over med.-low heat in oil until almost brown.
  3. Add shrimp and cook 15 mins, stirring occasionally
  4. Add parsley, shallots, and bell pepper; saute 10 mins., stirring.
  5. Add smoked sausage and cook for 10 mins.
  6. Add 3 cups water and cook for 1/2 hr. over med. heat.
  7. Add oysters and crab meat; cook 15 mins.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add rice little at a time, steadily stirring, until all rice is in.
  10. Heat over medium heat for 15 mins., stirring occasionally.
